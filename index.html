When did Tacos first appear in the United States ?

Tacos are believed to have first appeared in the United States in the late 19th century when Mexican immigrants introduced them in areas like the Southwest and California. Their popularity grew steadily throughout the 20th century specifically from 1905 eventually becoming a widely enjoyed and iconic American food.
